Hi! I am an undergraduate student at North Carolina State University. I am in the Learding Design and Technology program, and I will be graduating this summer, 2022.

Favorite Ed. Tech Tools

  1. Formative: I am especially fond of Formative because I personally believe it could be an online educational tool (learning management system) that could solely bridge the achievement gap between students of color and their white peers
  2. Padlet: I like Padlet because it allows individuals to collaborate in real time either under a username or annonymously if preferred.
  3. Blackboard: While Moodle is effective, I personally think the learning management system, Blackboard, is more efficient. It is easier to navigate and makes communicating with professors and peers a more seamless process all within the learning management system itself.
